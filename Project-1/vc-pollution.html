Impact snapshot — air, water, waste

Air: PM2.5 & Smog

Fine particulate matter penetrates lungs and bloodstream. Winter inversions + traffic + crop residue burning = toxic cocktail for metros and northern plains.

Water: Rivers & Plastics

Urban sewage, industrial effluents and microplastics stress rivers. Restoring wetlands and treating at source are critical for safe water.

Waste: Landfill Fires

Open dumping + methane build‑up spark fires that release dioxins. Source segregation, MRFs, and biomethanation change the game.

What drives pollution?

Rapid urbanisation, fossil fuel dependence, agricultural stubble burning, and weak waste chains combine to degrade air and water quality. Smart city planning can reverse this.

Transport — Congestion and diesel fleets spike NOx/PM. Shift to metros, buses, EVs, and last‑mile cycles.
Industry — Outdated boilers, coal reliance. Move to scrubbers, fuel switching, and energy efficiency.
Agriculture — Post‑harvest residue burning. Promote decomposers, straw markets, and happy seeders.
Waste — Mixed garbage = toxic legacy. Segregate at source and valorise organics.
